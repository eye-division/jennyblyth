Jenny Blyth Fine Art at CAREY BLYTH GALLERY (Open : Tues-Sat from 11-5 pm)

Located in the heart of Oxford at 8 Woodstock Road OX2 6HT, Carey Blyth Gallery shows contemporary art for sale and enjoyment by a stable of artists in solo and mixed exhibitions on a rolling programme. Click on Menu above to see images & info on individual artists.  See below for forthcoming and current projects.

'I find that I am chasing beauty and trust that you will find something beautiful in the gallery to savour for years to come.  I have a profound love of the natural world and much of the art that I exhibit is grounded in the tradition of landscape and still life whether abstracted or figurative.'   Jenny Blyth.

BACKGROUND : 

Jenny Blyth has thirty years experience in contemporary art as curator and gallerist. She was curator at Saatchi Gallery 1990-2002 and ran a floating gallery for ten years thereafter, originating projects and mounting exhibitions largely in London, Manchester and Oxford. Jenny Blyth Fine Art (JBFA) curated 40 exhibitions at Art Jericho in Oxford between 2010 and 2017, and opened Carey Blyth Gallery in September 2019. 

Recent curatorial projects include Seaside Citadels : Paintings by Day Bowman at Bermondsey Project Space, London SE1 (June 2021); Contemplation : Snow Into Water  (September/October 2020) and Touching the Earth (May 2019) at North Wall Arts Centre, Oxford; and an exhibition of photography by Jenny Blyth Reflections in the Landscape at Wolfson College, Oxford (February 2019).

Consultations for Artists :  Studio visits, crits and advice on work and path forward for artists, please Enquiries : jennyblyth@btconnect.com
