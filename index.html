Jenny Blyth Fine Art (JBFA), curator and floating gallerist, provides a fine art consultancy for a portfolio of artists and collectors providing advice, studio visits and critique, initiating projects, securing sales and curating exhibitions. 

If you are looking for unique contemporary art for your home or work place, see tabs above for images and info on individual artists :

Day Bowman has a lifetime of painting under her belt creating beautiful abstracted paintings that explore geopolitics, borders, boundaries and migration. Raised in the West Country on the coast her work is grounded in both the land and the outlying ocean as celebrated in her Seaside Citadels and Marking the Boundary series.

Chloe Fremantle is a true colourist composing visual haikus that have elements of synesthesia - a kaleidoscope of contemplations, evocations and reflections that invite us to travel down the rabbit hole.  Ever inspired by the beauty of nature, her mark making has become a jazz fusion of motifs and elements developed and balanced over the years.  Her compositions have perfect pitch on the canvas.

Anne Howeson is a story teller supreme, stepping across time from Georgian London to distopian days.  Drawing is her medium creating narrative laid over photography.  Haunting and unexpected, her compositions explore the avenues that interest her: the history of London, photography, the movement of people and relationships.  Her work around Kings Cross covers a 300 year transition from buccolic pastures to the futuristic urbanity that is Kings X today, a history of lost rivers and people passing through.  Volcanoes, her recent work, explores life and ghosts under Vesuvius where she has rural retreat.

Susan Moxley lives in both UK and Greece. Home to Aphrodite, her work is infused with the colours and the song of the island she loves. Her textile paintings tell stories of the culture and life that is changing fast as the younger generations embrace contemporary life. SM sees the island as a microcosm of what is happening in so many societies globally. Blacklisted by her homeland of South Africa, SM moved to Europe to make her life here. Politics remain core to her work.

Cassandra Wall creates textile paintings that capture the rhythms of the natural world. It is the play of light on water or the movement of saplings in woodlands, rather than the geography. She has long been captivated by the longshore drift.  Whether Grecian isles or UK coastlines, her tessellations and hand stitched textiles are beautiful coloured and composed, truly contemporary. 

Trish Wylie celebrates cinematography defying gender norms with her Desert Paintings. Her work is inspired by iconic film directors such Kazuo Ishiguro, John Ford and John Huston, and time she has spent alone in the Mojave Desert.   Her recent commissions include Joahchim Phoenix as Bonaparte, and Olivia Colman as The Queen.

JBFA CURATORIAL

Jenny Blyth has more than twenty five years experience in contemporary art as curator and gallerist. She was Curator at Saatchi Gallery 1990-2002 and curated 50 exhibitions at Art Jericho (2010-17) and Carey Blyth Gallery (2019-23).

In addition to exhibitions, mixed and solo, at Art Jericho and Carey Blyth Gallery, selected JBFA Curatorial Projects outside of CBG Gallery include :

Seaside Citadels : Paintings by Day Bowman                                                                  Bermondsey Project Space, London SE1 June 2021

Contemplation : Snow Into Water  September/October 2020                                                          Colour and Patterning in Art : Paintings by Chloe Fremantle; Textiles by Cassandra Wall at The North Wall Arts Centre, Oxford

CONSULTING & MARKETING (for Artists)

Studio visits, portfolio critiques and advice on work and path forward, originating and curating projects, securing mixed and solo exhibitions.

Text written and leaflets designed around your work - the process involves, image submissions, studio visit and interview, essay/text and brochure published.

If you would like to enquire about consulting or marketing please email for appointment/quote/view marketing materials email jennyblythart@gmail.com 

LECTURES / INDUSTRY SPECIALIST

In person or on line : Drawing on twenty five years of experience curating exhibitions, building collections, running galleries and projects etc. Jenny Blyth is available to give talks & lectures and hands on installation as Industry Expert. 

In March 2023 JB curated and hung the Student Exhibition at Morley College, London selecting 45 artworks from 90 submissions.  Pre-exhibition zoom lecture on Motivation and Practice for students, curated exhibition selected and hung, and a final Curatorial Talk and walk-around with all participating students (Foundation Yr and Yr 1, 2, 3 Undergraduates/Fine Art).
