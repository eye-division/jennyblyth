Jenny Blyth has twenty-five years background in contemporary art. She was curator at Saatchi Gallery 1990-2002 and ran a floating gallery for ten years thereafter, originating projects and mounting exhibitions largely in London, Manchester, Wolverhampton and Oxford. 

Jenny Blyth Fine Art curated 40 exhibitions at Art Jericho, Oxford from 2010-2017.

Since 2019 Jenny Blyth Fine Art has been based at Carey Blyth Gallery, 8 Woodstock Road, Oxford showing paintings, photography, sculpture by local, national and international artists.  The gallery shows solo and mixed exhibitions.

Jenny Blyth is also a photographer exploring the natural world.
