Jenny Blyth has twenty-five years background in contemporary art. She was curator at Saatchi Gallery 1990-2002 and ran a floating gallery for ten years thereafter, originating projects and mounting exhibitions largely in London and Oxford. Jenny Blyth Fine Art curated 40 exhibitions at Art Jericho since 2009 based at the gallery for three years from 2011.
